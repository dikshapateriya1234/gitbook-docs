# Publishing Your Documentation

## Publishing to the Web

1. Open your GitBook Space
2. Click Share at the top right
3. Toggle Publish to web ON
4. GitBook assigns a free URL like https://yourname.gitbook.io/your-space

## Visibility Settings

| Setting | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Public | Anyone can find and view it |
| Unlisted | Accessible via link but not indexed |
| Private | Only invited members can view |

Choose Public for maximum accessibility.

## Sharing Your URL

Once published, copy the URL from the Share panel and submit it. 
Your documentation is now live and accessible to anyone on the internet.

## Updating After Publishing

Every time you push changes to GitHub, your published GitBook site 
updates automatically within a few minutes. No need to republish manually.
